Subject: Water Supply and Sewage Management Regulations
Department: Water Supply and Drainage Authority (WSDA)

Keywords: Water Leakage, Pipe Burst, Drainage Block, Sewage Overflow, Blocked Line, Water Pipe.

The Water Supply and Drainage Authority (WSDA) handles all matters related to the city's water infrastructure. 
This department manages public water lines, sewage systems, and storm drains.

Complaints regarding water leakage from public pipes, including large pipe bursts, must be immediately flagged for the WSDA. 
Similarly, any report of a major drainage block leading to sewage overflow or surface flooding is the responsibility of the Water Supply 
and Drainage Authority.
