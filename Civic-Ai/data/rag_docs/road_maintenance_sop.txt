Subject: Roads and Infrastructure Repair Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
Department: Roads and Highways Department

Keywords: Pothole, Road Damage, Asphalt, Surface Cracks, Road Safety.

The Roads and Highways Department is responsible for maintaining all public roadways and ensuring driver safety. 
This includes all forms of road surface damage.

A pothole is classified as a high-priority repair item and must be logged for immediate assessment. Any complaint specifically
detailing a pothole or other severe asphalt damage is directly routed to the Roads and Highways Department.
