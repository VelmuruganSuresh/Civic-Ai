Subject: Public Lighting and Electrical Infrastructure Maintenance
Department: City Electrical Board

Keywords: Street Light Issue, Faulty Light, Electrical Pole, Broken Bulb, Non-Functional Light.

The City Electrical Board is the sole authority for managing public lighting infrastructure.
 All reports related to a street light issue, including non-functional, flickering, or damaged lights, are the jurisdiction of the 
 City Electrical Board.

The City Electrical Board also addresses potential hazards from damaged electrical poles and exposed wiring in public spaces.
