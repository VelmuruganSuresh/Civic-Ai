Subject: Waste Management and Public Health Response Guidelines
Department: Municipal Sanitation and Public Health Department

Keywords: Garbage, Waste, Dead Animal, Carcass, Foul Smell, Overflowing Bins.

The Municipal Sanitation and Public Health Department is responsible for all aspects of waste management within city limits. 
This includes the scheduled collection and disposal of garbage, as well as managing unauthorized dumping sites.

The removal of a dead animal or carcass from public and private property is a critical public health function and is also 
solely managed by the Municipal Sanitation and Public Health Department. All such complaints must be addressed with priority due to 
the health risk and **foul smell they present.
